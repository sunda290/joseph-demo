A new home for your work

Your photographs, in your clients’ hands.

A brand-new website, built from the ground up: a private, branded space where the people you photograph can view their images and order prints or digital files — opened with a single QR code on a session card. Paired with a public site designed to get your work found on Google by new clients in Hamilton.

01

The idea

After a shoot, every client receives a small card with a QR code. They scan it and land in a gallery that looks and feels like your brand — clean, monochrome, your name on every frame. They choose what they love and buy it on the spot. No emails back and forth, no file transfers, no chasing payments.

02

What your clients experience

  • Scan the QR code on their session card
  • Enter a private gallery, branded to you
  • Browse watermarked previews of their photographs
  • Buy digital files or fine-art prints — by size
  • Receive instant high-res downloads; prints shipped to the door
03

What it does for you

  • A separate gallery for each session or event
  • A QR code generated automatically for every gallery
  • Payments handled by Stripe — funds land in your account
  • Galleries stay private — only the QR or link opens them
A working prototype is already built. You can walk through the full client journey — and your studio side, with the QR codes — before a single decision is made.
See it live → joseph-demo.vercel.app
04

Getting found on Google

The client galleries above are private and stay out of search — that’s by design. Being found on Google is a separate, public job, and here’s what actually moves the needle for a local photographer:

  • Google Business Profile — the single biggest lever for “photographer Hamilton” and “near me” searches. Puts you on the map, with photos and reviews.
  • A public portfolio site built for search — a dedicated page per service, fast-loading, mobile-first.
  • Local keywords — Hamilton, Ontario, headshots, portraits — written into real page text, not just images.
  • Reviews & local citations — to earn Google’s trust that you’re the real, local choice.
05

How we’d roll it out

Phase 1
Be found. Tune the public site for Google and set up the Business Profile, so new clients in Hamilton can discover you.
Phase 2
Sell effortlessly. Launch the private client galleries, QR codes, and checkout — turning every shoot into easy print & digital sales.

This is a complete rebuild — a brand-new site engineered from the ground up on a fast, modern foundation that is fully yours. The only thing carried over from the current site is your photography; everything else is designed fresh around the way you actually work.
